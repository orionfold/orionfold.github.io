A year ago, the best AI was closed. You paid to use it, and it ran in someone else's cloud. Open models trailed far behind.

That gap has nearly closed.

## What changed

Open models are free to download and run on your own machine. On public tests, where every model answers the same questions, the best open ones now land within a few points of the closed ones. For most real work, that is close enough.

## When closed still wins

We are honest about this. For the very hardest tasks, the top closed models still lead. If you need that edge and the data is not private, a paid service can be the right call.

## Our bet

But for work where privacy, cost, and control matter, open wins. You own the model. You run it offline. You pay no fee per person. That is the future we are building for.
