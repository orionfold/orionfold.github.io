We started Orionfold with one belief. AI is changing how work gets done, and the people who learn to build with it will pull ahead.

But most AI today lives behind a paywall, in someone else's cloud. Your data goes out. A fee comes back for every person who uses it. That felt wrong for the kind of work we care about, where privacy and trust matter most.

So we folded our own studio. The name is a nod to that idea: take big, open AI and fold it down to run on a small desktop, right where you work.

## What we build

We make the full kit. Open software to run AI helpers on your own machine. Custom models trained for one field, like patents or medicine. And the guides to put it all to use.

## Why build in public

We learn fast when we share. Every week we ship something, write down what we learned, and post it here. Some of it works. Some of it breaks. All of it is honest.

Come along. The best way to learn AI is to watch it get built, step by step.
