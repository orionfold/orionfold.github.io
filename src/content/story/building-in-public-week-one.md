Week one is done. Here is the honest log.

## What we shipped

We put up the first version of this site. It shows our books, our software, and our models, with a clear link to each one. Plain words up top, the deep detail tucked behind a click for anyone who wants it.

## What broke

Our first build of the model pages mixed up two file types and pointed a few links to the wrong place. We caught it by checking every link by hand against the real pages. Slow, but it works.

## What we learned

Small and clear beats big and clever. We cut three fancy ideas this week and the site got better each time. We will keep cutting.

Next week we wire up real stories, like this one, so the blog reads from our own writing instead of stand-in text.
